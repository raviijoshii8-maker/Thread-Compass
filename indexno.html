Slow Fashion, Fast Patina.

In an era of disposable fashion, Thread & Compass stands as a monument to the enduring. We source our textiles from the few remaining family-owned mills in Okayama, Biella, and Chicago.

Every stitch is considered. Every button is selected for its ability to age. We don't believe in "new" looks; we believe in the beauty of a jacket that has seen five years of rain and sun.
